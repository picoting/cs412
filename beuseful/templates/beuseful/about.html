About BUseful

Welcome to BUseful, a platform designed to connect students in need of services with peers who offer those services. Whether you're looking for help with tutoring, repairs, or creative tasks, or you're a skilled seller looking to offer your talents, this site is your go-to marketplace.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster collaboration and entrepreneurship within student communities. We aim to:

  • Provide a platform for students to monetize their skills and talents.
  • Offer affordable and reliable services to students in need.
  • Encourage a culture of mutual support and cooperation among peers.

How It Works

  1. Sign up and create your profile.
  2. Browse services offered by sellers or register as a seller to list your own services.
  3. Place orders and track their progress in real-time.
